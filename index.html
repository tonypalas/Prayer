My Prayer

My prayer is that as we draw closer to Christ's return, His bride will draw closer to Him! May His church be without spot or wrinkle, in one accord, giving Him all the glory for what He has done. Holy Spirit, call us to our knees—our rightful battle position—as we fight to advance Your kingdom in our lives and around us. Use this time of prayer and fasting to purify my heart and align my spirit with Your will. Amen.
